How it works (technical detail)

The script creates an RTCPeerConnection with a list of public STUN servers, starts an offer, and listens for icecandidate events. Each candidate that arrives is parsed to extract the IP — these are the IPs your browser is willing to advertise for peer-to-peer connections, including local network addresses and your true public IP if mDNS obfuscation is off.
